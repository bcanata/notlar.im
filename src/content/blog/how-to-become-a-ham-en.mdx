To get the Amateur Radio Certificate I'd been interested in for a while, I sat Turkey's Amateur Radio Exam. The exam fee is 115 TL, and after the 2021 price increase the amateur radio certificate (license) fee is 75 TL for students and 150 TL for non-students. In this video I gathered the step-by-step details of the exam — how to get the certificate, what to watch out for — together with the new exam process information announced in November 2020. You can use it as a tidy visual resource for your application.
