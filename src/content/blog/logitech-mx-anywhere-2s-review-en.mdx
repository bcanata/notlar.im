Logitech's mouse that works both over Bluetooth and with its own Unifying USB receiver. Between Bluetooth and USB it can pair with a total of 3 devices. There's a tiny button on the bottom with 1-2-3 indicator lights — that's how you switch between them.

It's been about 2 years now, and I use it daily. Its only problem: the side pieces — right where you grip it in your hand — swelled up and peeled off. I solved that by [printing replacements in TPU on a 3D printer](https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:6119699). Details are in the [useful 3D print projects](/en/blog/useful-3d-prints-en) post.
