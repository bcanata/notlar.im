### IMPORTANT WARNING

  
If a firmware update from your computer gets interrupted halfway and throws an error, do **NOT TURN THE RADIO OFF**. Close the program you were using to flash the firmware and start it again. There's a button labelled "Reset" — press it to reset the half-finished firmware flash. If you power the radio off before the update completes, your radio will be "bricked" and become unusable. In that case you would have to replace the radio's memory chip.
