A list of popular public torrent sites grouped by what they're best at.

### 1) [The Pirate Bay](https://thepiratebay10.org/) — most-used in general

The Pirate Bay is one of the most-known and most-comprehensive torrent sites. It's been the subject of many legal cases over the years; shut down, reopened. Today it's still online via several alternative domains.

### 2) [RARBG](https://rarbg.to/index70.php) — best for new releases and 4K

Another site that gets blocked from time to time. Content is moderated and approved, so you're less likely to run into Pirate-Bay-style surprises.

### 3) [1337x](https://1337x.to/) — best for software / apps

Founded in 2007 but became popular especially in 2018 when several other torrent sites shut down. Banned from Google search results so you can't reach it via Google. Note: their ads can be NSFW, not ideal for the office or for children's machines.

### 4) [Torlock](https://www.torlock.com/movies/1/size/desc.html) — one of the better ones for ebooks and anime

A site with a clean UI and a rich ebook/anime catalogue. Some torrents you can't find elsewhere show up here. Worth noting Torlock also goes a bit overboard with ads.

### 5) [Torrentz2](https://torrentz2eu.org/) — strong on music

Torrentz2 has a particularly good music album catalogue. Simple UI.

### 6) [Zoogle](https://zooqle.com/) — strong on games

One of the newer torrent sites; mostly known for games but you can find movies and series too.

---

Worth noting that while these sites are pretty good for international content, they're a bit thin on Turkish content. The main reason is that Turkish rights-holders monitor these channels closely and send takedown requests.
