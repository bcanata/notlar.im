A series of travel guides covering many countries — perhaps among the most successful in the field. I learned quite a lot even from their Turkey guide. They've written excellent write-ups for archaeological sites here that have no guide or signage of their own.

- [Lonely Planet Turkey — Amazon Türkiye](https://www.amazon.com.tr/Lonely-Planet-Turkey-Travel-Guide-dp-178657800X/dp/178657800X/ref=dp_ob_title_bk)

Unfortunately, because of the exchange rate the price here in Turkey has gone through the roof; you can get the e-book versions from Z-Library.
