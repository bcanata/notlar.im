Designs I've printed for myself and found useful.

- **Side grip parts for the Logitech MX Anywhere 2S.** The [soft side pieces](https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:6119699) of the mouse I use. The originals deteriorated in the sun and came off; I printed replacements in TPU.
- **A quick-to-print whistle that sounds good** — [Thingiverse #3130078](https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:3130078). I've printed it in both ABS and PLA.
- **Magnetic SD / micro-SD card case** — [Printables #464786](https://www.printables.com/model/464786-slim-magnetic-sd-micro-sd-card-case).
- **Holder for two batteries + two SD cards for the Canon M50** — [Thingiverse #3566796](https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:3566796).
- **Two-battery holder for the Canon M50** — [Thingiverse #5403046](https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:5403046).
- **Hot shoe cover for the Canon M50** — [Printables #226558](https://www.printables.com/model/226558-hot-shoe-cover-minimalistic-internal-flash-working).
- **Trophy set** — a two-part [trophy set](https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:5248363) made of a base and a top piece. I print these for my students.
