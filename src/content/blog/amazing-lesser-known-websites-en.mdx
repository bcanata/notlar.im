The version on Ekşi Sözlük (a Turkish collaborative dictionary site) turned into garbage, so let's continue here.

- [notebooklm.google](https://notebooklm.google/) — Google's AI-powered notebook. You can add PDFs and other files, and when you ask it something it answers with citations to your sources.
- [fccid.io](https://fccid.io/) — A site collecting the licensing records of the FCC, the US Federal Communications Commission. From the documents submitted for devices approved for sale, you can even see photos of a device's internals.
- [free-for.dev](https://free-for.dev/) — Lists free resources for developers.
- [recraft.ai](https://app.recraft.ai/) — A vector-generation app. It creates the logo you describe as a vector, and you can export it as SVG.
- [foldnfly.com](https://www.foldnfly.com/) — Instructions for folding paper airplanes in different designs.
- [onlinedoctranslator.com](https://www.onlinedoctranslator.com/en/translationform) — Translates PDF, DOC and similar files online, powered by Google Translate.
- [secondlifestorage.com cell database](https://secondlifestorage.com/index.php?pages/cell-database/) — A database of 18650 batteries. Helps you figure out which battery is which model.
- [standardebooks.org](https://standardebooks.org/) — Collects and publishes e-books that are out of copyright.
- [domainr.com](https://domainr.com/) — A domain search tool that also shows domain-hack style short names.
- [tld-list.com](https://tld-list.com/) — Shows which registrars sell/renew each TLD at the cheapest price.
- [elevenlabs.io](https://elevenlabs.io/) — Converts text to speech with AI. Supports Turkish too.
- [lookscanned.io](https://lookscanned.io/scan) — Makes an uploaded document look like it went through a scanner. Handy for making files signed on a tablet look "scanned".
- [annas-archive.org](https://annas-archive.org/) — An e-book site mirroring Sci-Hub, LibGen, Z-Library and the like.
- [geospy.web.app](https://geospy.web.app/) — Uses AI to guess the location where a photo was taken.
- [whatsmyname.app](https://whatsmyname.app/) — Finds social media accounts registered under the username you type.
- [noadexchange.com](https://noadexchange.com/) — A Warmshowers-style site. You host guests Airbnb-style and earn credits, then use those credits to stay in other cities and countries. There's a one-time $75 cleaning fee, and that goes to the host.
- [htmldocs.com](https://htmldocs.com/) — Lets you generate PDFs with HTML. There's an API service too; you can assign variables to certain parts of the PDF and generate dynamic PDFs.
- [archivebuttons.com](https://www.archivebuttons.com/) — Gathers the paywall-bypassing services under one roof.
- [nearbywiki.org](https://nearbywiki.org/) — Shows the locations of Wikipedia pages on a map. Useful for finding places worth seeing wherever you go.
- [punchsalad.com SSL generator](https://punchsalad.com/ssl-certificate-generator/) — A Let's Encrypt certificate generator. Works for free.
- [libble.eu](https://www.libble.eu/) — An archive of user manuals.
- [pfpmaker.com](https://pfpmaker.com/) — A profile picture generator.
- [reddit-soccerstreams.com](https://www.reddit-soccerstreams.com/) — Lists links to football match streams.
- [maps.nimbo.earth](https://maps.nimbo.earth/) — A map service updated monthly. Gives you 60,000 free credits.
- [imagetotext.io photo-translate](https://www.imagetotext.io/photo-translate) — Translates text from photos.
- [flightlist.io](https://www.flightlist.io/) — A flight search engine.
- [v0.dev](https://v0.dev/) — Vercel's AI interface-design app.
- [linkpreview.xyz](https://linkpreview.xyz/) — A link-preview viewer for developers. Lets you check how a link will look on WhatsApp, Twitter, etc.
- [blocks.md](https://blocks.md/) — A Typeform-alternative form builder. Turns Markdown into forms.
