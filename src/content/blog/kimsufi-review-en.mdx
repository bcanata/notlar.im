A hosting company that offers service on older servers within OVH.

I've been using the KS-1 and KS-2 plans since roughly 2014. Pricing is 5 and 10 euros. I set up [Caprover](https://caprover.com/) to run a seedbox and handle my odd bits and pieces. They give you a 100 Mbit connection with no bandwidth cap. And you don't share it with the rest of the rack — it's all yours. They don't hassle you about what you do on the server with "fair use" clauses and the like.

That said, customer support is slow. In these 10 years I've only needed them once or twice, for billing.

You can track which servers are in stock at [checkservers.ovh](https://checkservers.ovh/).
