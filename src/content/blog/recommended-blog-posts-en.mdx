Blog posts I've come across here and there — ones worth reading, ones I've read and liked, ones I want to read.

- [sive.rs/com](https://sive.rs/com) — explains how to download TLD zone files through ICANN's CZDS system and filter out the unregistered domains.
- [halityesil.com — How not to write PHP code](https://halityesil.com/php/phpde-kod-nasil-yazilmaz-baslica-hatalar-ve-ipuclari/) — a nice article (in Turkish) about common mistakes in PHP.
- [wiki-power.com — Building a HomeLab](https://wiki-power.com/en/%E6%90%AD%E5%BB%BA%E5%B1%9E%E4%BA%8E%E8%87%AA%E5%B7%B1%E7%9A%84HomeLab/) — a wiki that collects everything in one place, similar to notlar.im. This page and the ones that follow cover self-hosting.
- [blog.haschek.at — The curious case of the RasPi](https://blog.haschek.at/2019/the-curious-case-of-the-RasPi-in-our-network.html) — he found a Raspberry Pi in the server room and, by following the clues, tracked down the person who planted it. An interesting story.
