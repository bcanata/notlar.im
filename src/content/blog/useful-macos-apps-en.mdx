macOS apps I use and find useful.

- [Reeder](https://reederapp.com/) — an RSS reader, also available on iOS devices.
- [TablePlus](https://tableplus.com/) — a database management app that supports a whole bunch of databases. The free version allows 2 open tabs, which is usually enough.
- [Upscayl](https://upscayl.org/) — an open-source (AI-based) image upscaler.
